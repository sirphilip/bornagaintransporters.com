Services for you

For the past few years now Born Again Transporters has been the lead in the transport sector. It has clearly dominated the market all around Kirinyaga county and and is also taking the place around its environs.

We are proud to note that once we provide our services to our various clients, we make them feel as cormfortable working with us as possible. Once clients work with us, they highly recommend our services to the other companies and individuals who are planning to start a construction. We are never so quick to demand for pay until our clients acertain that they are fully satisfied with our product and services.

In the case of customer's dissatisfaction, they can freely report the issue at our headquarters. The issue is clearly recorded and the management team looks into it in detail. Neccesary actions are taken to ensure our customers are satisfied.
Suppy of Building materials
We supply our customers with the neccessary requirements for building purposes. Such include sand, building stones of various types, ballast, murram and other products required for building.
Supply hardware equipments
We provide hardware parts and equipments required for construction of buildings. Examples of such parts include cement, iron sheets, steel wires, binding wires, nails, paint and many more.
Contractual services
We also work on contract basis. For instance our trucks are posted on a certain location till the completion of certain assigned contract.
Hiring purposes
We provide our vehicles for hire. This can be in the case where our vehicles are hired by an individual or a company. In such a case we charge the transport fee only
Tender services
The county government of Kirinyaga has recognized the quality of the services we offer and has since been giving us the chance to supply murram and quarry waste to repair rural roads among other duties.
Provision of building workforce
Born Again Transporters company is also glad to inform you that they can now provide the relevant machinery and equipment required for building. On the other hand, we also provide architects, marsons, carpenters and even plumbers.
For a long time now around kirinyaga county, we have been the leading company in provision of quality services and we have worn ourselves various tenders to supply the various services related to constructing buildings. The tenders include provision of materials in schools(both public and private), in hospitals and in construction of supermarkets.

Welcome

Born Again Transporters is a transportation and delivery service company. We deal with transportation of building materials. The company was started as a small firm in the year 1985 by the current M.D Andrew Mwati. It initially had 5 employees and it has now successfully grown to a large company of over 50 workers.

Born Again Transporters, is all about quality and durability of building materials. We ensure that we give the best quality of our products and services and enhance 100% guarantee customer's satisfaction upon delivery.
