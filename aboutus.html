Company Information

Fifteen years ago, this company had been known for only provision of building materials. These including supply of different types of stones such as 4*9, 6*9 and 9*9 sizes, supply of the various qualities of sand such as river sand and 'special' sand.

Over the last decade, we have been able to expand our company and hence we have been in a position to provide much more services and products as compared to the past years.
We have extensively grown wide in the transportation sector. We have improved our delivery systems and we have also digitalised the way we have been serving our esteemed customers.

Welcome

Born Again Transporters is a transportation and delivery service company. We deal with transportation of building materials. The company was started as a small firm in the year 1985 by the current M.D Andrew Mwati. It initially had 5 employees and it has now successfully grown to a large company of over 50 workers.

Born Again Transporters, is all about quality and durability of building materials. We ensure that we give the best quality of our products and services and enhance 100% guarantee customer's satisfaction upon delivery.
